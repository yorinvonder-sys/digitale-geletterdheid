DGSkills Security Policy

If you discover a security issue in DGSkills, report it responsibly to security@dgskills.app.

Response SLA: we aim to acknowledge reports within 48 hours and patch critical issues within 7 days.

Scope

  • https://dgskills.app
  • https://www.dgskills.app

Out of Scope

  • Social engineering attacks
  • Physical attacks
  • Denial of service attacks
  • Attacks requiring physical access to user devices

Disclosure Guidelines

  • Do not publicly disclose the issue before a fix is available.
  • Do not access data that does not belong to your own account.
  • Do not degrade service availability for users.
